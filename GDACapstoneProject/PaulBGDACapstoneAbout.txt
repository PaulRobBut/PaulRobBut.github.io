This is my Google Data Analytics Capstone project, focusing on Case Study 1, the Cyclistic bike share project. In this project, I utilized Google Sheets and RStudio to analyze two datasets of quarterly data to determine how Casual and Member riders utilize their bikes in the way that they do. Through this, I uncovered where they are similar, where they differ, and even one area that caused the data results to give inaccurate results. The project is designed to utilize a text document and a slideshow in tandem to form a nonverbal (though it can be verbal if need be) presentation of what the data results are and why I came to the conclusions that I did.
